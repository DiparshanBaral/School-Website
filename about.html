About Us

Welcome to Amar English School, where learning meets excellence!

Established with a vision to provide quality education and create a nurturing environment for young minds, Amar English School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Devchuli-16, Rajahar, Nawalparasi, Nepal.

At Amar English School, we believe in fostering holistic development by nurturing the intellectual, emotional, and physical well-being of our students. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a stimulating learning environment that encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for learning.

With a focus on academic excellence, character development, and extracurricular activities, we strive to equip our students with the necessary skills, knowledge, and values to thrive in an ever-evolving global society.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that blends academic excellence with practical learning experiences, enabling students to explore their interests and talents across various disciplines. Our classrooms are equipped with modern technology, providing students with a dynamic and interactive learning environment.

At Amar English School, we value diversity, inclusivity, and mutual respect. We celebrate the uniqueness of each student, encouraging them to embrace their individuality and develop into compassionate global citizens.

We invite you to join our school community, where every student is given the opportunity to discover their potential, pursue their passions, and embark on a journey of lifelong learning.
